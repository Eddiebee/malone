Adopting is one of the best things you can do.

When you adopt, you're not just giving a deserving dog a home. You're also reducing the demand for puppy mills and other industrial-scale breeding that put profit before animal welfare.

Meet Our Pups

Pedro

Calm and wise, Pedro is the perfect companion for an older individual looking for a less active experience. Responds well to commands and has a gorgeous, lustrous coat.

Jasmine

If you have young kids. Jasmine's the perfect dog. Part playmate, part nanny, she'll tire them out while keeping a watchful eye on everything. She also loves to cuddle at night!

Michael

Intelligient, alert and a bit mischeivous, Michael is always up for an adventure. Whether you're jumping in the car for a weekend road trip or going for a run, he'll be by your side.

Phillipe

Phillipe is one of the most soulful dogs we've ever had in our care. Though shy, he's capable of deep connection and is in search of a family who will love and appreciate him.

Dominic

If it's tricks you're after, Dominic is your dog. He's a pro at running, fetching and catching a frisbee mid-flight. We can't imagine a better companion for an active couple.

Tia

Tia is all about relaxation and is looking for someone who enjoys naps, lounging on the couch and gentle evening walks. She's diabetic but we'll work you through her care routine.

Atimnne

Atimnne is diligent and purposeful. She'll be of great inspiration and motivation to an active fellow, she'll make sure you're up and doing, and also a rest-time freak. If you're looking for a work partner, one to get the plate, get the pawn and search for the missing ball, Atimnne got you covered.
